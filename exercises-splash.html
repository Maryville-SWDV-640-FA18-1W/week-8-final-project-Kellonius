Muscle Groups

As you workout, keep in mind that each muscle group requires differing levels of attention. For instance, forearms, abdominals and calves can be exercised twice as much as other muscle groups.

A mistake that most beginners make is that they focus on the "money muscles" - the biceps and pectorals. If legs and lower body is neglected long enough, you might begin to look top heavy and disproportionate.

Workout of the day

Bicep curls - 3 sets of 8 to 10 reps at 80% of max weight.

100 meter sprint for time.

Tricep curls - 3 sets of 8 to 10 reps at 80% of max weight.

100 meter sprint for time.

Overhand curls - 3 sets of 4 to 8 reps at 90% of max weight.

400 meter jog for recovery.

Skullcrushers - 3 sets of 12 of more reps at 75% of max weight.

100 meter sprint for time.

800 meter walk/jog for cool down.
