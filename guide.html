Suggest Workout

Start with low weight. On day 1, do several different exercises to determine how much weight you can comfortably lift for 12 repetitions.

Once you have found a comfortable weight, follow this schedule for each workout, increasing weight by 2 to 5 lbs each week.

Week 1: 3 sets of 12 reps
Week 2: 3 sets of 10 reps
Week 3: 3 sets of 8 reps
Week 4: 4 sets of 6 reps
Week 5: 4 sets of 5 reps
Week 6: Set 1 - 12 reps, Set 2 - 10 reps, Set 3 - 8 reps, Set 4 - 6 reps, Set 5 - 5 reps

For abs, do this routine as recommended:

Captains Chair leg raise
Hanging leg raise
Ab-wheel rollout
Air bicycle
Crunches
Cable crunches

For cardio, do this exercise once for every two days of weight lifting exercises:

Incline treadmill sprints - 5 intervals for 30 seconds at 90-100% exertion with 3-4 minute rest intervals
Cycling - 7 intervals for 60 seconds at 90-100% exertion with 2-3 minute active recovery
Jump rope - 10 intervals for 45 seconds at 90-100% exertion with 2 minute rest
